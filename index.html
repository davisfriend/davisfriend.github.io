KIDNAP Official

Kiefer Napolitano

KIDNAP is the musical project of 20 year-old Kiefer Napolitano from the New Orleans, Louisiana area. KIDNAP released his first project, Ambidextrous, in the summer of 2017. His follow up, Bright Colors, was released in the winter of 2018. After releasing the Heavens Drive EP with fellow Mandeville, Louisiana collective SWAD (Started With A Dream) on all streaming services in early 2019, KIDNAP spent the rest of the year working with local producers to find a unique sound for himself that will be displayed on his upcoming untitled project.
